  • Learn a modern JavaScript framework (React recommended — strong job market demand)
  • Build 1–2 new projects using React and deploy them (Netlify/Vercel)

  • Learn backend basics: Node.js + Express, plus a database (MongoDB or MySQL)

Phase 4 · 6+ months · Specialization & Portfolio Polish
  • Choose a specialization direction: Frontend Development OR Digital Marketing/Content (or both, as a "developer with marketing skills")
  • Refresh portfolio with full-stack and framework-based projects
  • Begin applying for internships/junior roles with a stronger, framework-backed portfolio
