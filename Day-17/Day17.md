🚗 Day 17/60 — Vehicle Cost Analysis Report

Today I explored how fuel choices impact vehicle running costs using data analysis and visualization techniques.

For this project, I analyzed the fuel economics of a Toyota Corolla in Pakistan, comparing Petrol, Diesel, CNG, and EV options based on cost per kilometer, monthly fuel expenses, and potential savings.

📊 Key Findings:
• Petrol Cost: PKR 24.6/km
• CNG Cost: PKR 13.3/km
• EV Cost: PKR 7.0/km
• Potential Annual Savings with CNG: PKR 135,000+
