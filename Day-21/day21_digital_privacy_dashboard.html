Privacy Improvement Plan
Actionable Steps, Prioritized
Recommended actions sorted by impact and ease
🔴 URGENT
Enable 2FA on Google, Meta, and Amazon
Your Google account controls search, payments, and photos. A breach here is catastrophic. Enable 2FA immediately using an authenticator app (not SMS).
🟠 HIGH
Audit TikTok Privacy Settings
Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Sharing and limit all third-party sharing. Consider switching to private account mode to reduce content-based profiling.
🟠 HIGH
Remove Location Access from Non-Essential Apps
Snapchat, Instagram, Discord — none need your precise location to function. Set all to "Never" or "While Using" in your phone's location settings.
🟡 MEDIUM
Review Google Photos Face Grouping
In Google Photos, go to Settings → Group Similar Faces and disable if you prefer your facial data not to be indexed and organized by Google's recognition models.
🟢 NICE
Use iMessage & Signal Instead of WhatsApp
iMessage is already in your stack and encrypts by default. For Android contacts, Signal offers equal privacy. Reduces Meta's reach over your communications.
