06

Market Gap Analysis

1
No student-first onboarding on existing platforms
Upwork & Fiverr require proven experience to win first jobs — a catch-22 for students with no portfolio or reviews.
2
Zero Urdu/Pashto language support
Global platforms are English-only, excluding millions of talented students in KPK, Balochistan, and rural Punjab.
3
No local payment rails for students
International platforms require Payoneer or USD bank accounts — almost impossible for under-18 students in Pakistan.
4
Micro-project economy is underserved
Small local businesses want $10–$50 micro-tasks; no platform caters to this Pakistani micro-gig segment at scale.
5
No mentorship-to-gig pipeline
Students need guided, supervised early gig experience. No platform pairs mentors with student freelancers in Pakistan.
