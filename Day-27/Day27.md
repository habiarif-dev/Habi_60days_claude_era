Day 27/60 — I built an interactive healthcare story simulator using Claude AI 🏥

Today's project: Prior Authorization Story Simulator — an 8-scene interactive story following Rahul through the full PA journey with StarCare Health.

What the app covers:
🩺 Doctor diagnosis → Humira prescription
🚧 PA submission flow (Provider → Payer, no pharmacy)
📚 What PA actually is — in plain English
🔍 The 4 checkpoints insurers review
❌ Denial — and why it's not final
✉️ The appeal + Letter of Medical Necessity
✅ Approval with a permanent reference number
📊 System metrics: denial rate, appeal rate, resolution time

You choose 1 of 2 options after every scene — restart to explore different paths.

Built in a single HTML file. No external dependencies. Typing indicators, animated chat bubbles, character avatars, a live progress bar.

Healthcare education meets interactive storytelling. 💡
