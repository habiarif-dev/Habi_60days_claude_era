Day 29/60 of the ABTalks AI Mastery Challenge 🚨

Today I built Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab — a full 8-phase interactive simulation where you manage a real corporate supply chain crisis from start to finish.

In this simulator you:
✅ Get a randomly generated company (industry, revenue, suppliers, factories)
⚠️ Face one of 8 crises (factory fire, cyberattack, port strike & more)
⚔️ Choose 3 crisis response actions in the War Room
🤝 Negotiate with suppliers across 4 branching rounds
🏛️ Answer 5 CEO leadership questions
🤖 Select 2 AI investments to prevent the next crisis
📊 Get scored across Leadership, Negotiation, Resilience & more

Every playthrough is different. No two crises are the same.

Built with: React (CDN) + Babel JSX — single HTML file, runs offline.
