🚀 Day 3 of the ABTalks 60-Day Claude AI Mastery Challenge

Today I explored the power of Role-Based Prompting — one of the simplest yet most effective ways to get better results from AI.

Instead of asking AI generic questions, assigning a specific role (Teacher, Business Consultant, Content Writer, etc.) provides context, perspective, and expertise, leading to more accurate and actionable responses.

💡 Key Takeaways: ✅ More relevant and focused answers ✅ Better decision-making support ✅ Saves time by reducing back-and-forth prompts

The quality of AI outputs often depends on the quality of the instructions we provide. A small change in prompting can create a huge difference in results.
