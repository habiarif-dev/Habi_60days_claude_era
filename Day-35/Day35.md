Meet **Prompt Puzzle** — an interactive game that teaches you how to write better AI prompts by *playing* with them. Build prompts by dragging the right blocks, clean bloated over-engineered prompts down to what actually matters, and pick the best prompt out of three lookalikes.

Today's build focused on Web Development & AI scenarios — responsive navbars, chatbot widgets, form validation, AI alt-text generation, and more. Complete with live scoring, a Prompt DNA breakdown, and a full performance report at the end. 🎯

Turns out the best way to learn prompting isn't reading about it — it's getting your wrong placements roasted by a scoreboard. 😅
