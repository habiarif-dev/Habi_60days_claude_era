🧠 Day 36/60 — Cognitive Pattern Explorer
Today's build: a psychology-inspired self-reflection tool that helps you discover your thinking patterns — not through a test, but through real-life scenarios, priority mapping, and guided reflection.
✨ 3 immersive chapters: Discover Your Thinking Style → Choose Your Priorities → Map Your Thinking
🌤️ Calm Mode & Stress Mode to explore how pressure shifts your cognition
📊 A personalized Reflection Journal with a visual breakdown of your thinking tendencies
💯 Single-file HTML, vanilla JS, fully offline, no frameworks
The goal isn't to label you — it's to help you notice your patterns so you can grow. Analytical, intuitive, overthinker, action-first, or balanced... which one sounds like you? 👇
