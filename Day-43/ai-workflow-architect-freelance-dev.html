01 Workflow Summary

This pipeline takes a freelance web/app developer from an inbound lead to a signed-off, invoiced project across 10 stages: Discovery & Proposal, Scoping & Contract, Project Setup & Planning, Design & Prototyping, Development, Testing & QA, Client Review & Revisions, Deployment & Launch, Handoff & Documentation, and Post-Launch Support. Each stage compresses research, writing, and repetitive technical work with AI so more time goes to architecture decisions, client relationships, and code quality — the parts that are hard to automate.
