Day 47 of #60DaysOfAI 🚀
Built "Content Intelligence Studio" — an AI-style content consultant that lives entirely in a single HTML file. No API, no server, no login.
Paste your post, script, or article (or drop in a thumbnail/screenshot), and it runs a multi-stage review — hook strength, clarity, platform fit, engagement design, SEO — then hands you a rewrite, alternative hooks, a publishing checklist, and a before/after score comparison.
The bigger lesson from this challenge: you don't always need a backend to make something feel intelligent — good structure + thoughtful heuristics can go a long way.
