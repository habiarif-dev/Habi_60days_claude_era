Day 48 of #60DaysOfAI: I built a decision engine that shows its work.
Most "compare & decide" tools just spit out a ranking. This one makes you the judge — adjust the weight of every criterion and watch the verdict update live. But the part I'm proud of isn't the sliders. It's that every data point is either cited to a real source, or explicitly flagged as an estimate. When two sources disagreed (DigitalOcean's ease of use, Dell XPS 13's battery life), I didn't pick a winner quietly — I documented the conflict.
Zero APIs. Zero libraries. One HTML file, running entirely in your browser.
