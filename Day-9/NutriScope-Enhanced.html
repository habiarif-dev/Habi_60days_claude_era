⚠️ Limitations & Caveats
• Nutritional values are averages from food composition tables and may vary by brand, preparation method, ripeness, and cooking technique.
• RDA values used are based on ICMR 2020 guidelines for Indian adults. Values differ for children, pregnant/lactating women, elderly, and those with medical conditions.
• Bioavailability (actual absorption) varies significantly. For example, non-haem iron from plant foods is absorbed at 2–20%, versus 15–35% for haem iron from meat.
• Vitamin D values from food alone are rarely sufficient; most humans rely heavily on sunlight synthesis.
• This tool does not account for drug-nutrient interactions, genetic variants (e.g. MTHFR for folate), or clinical malabsorption conditions.
